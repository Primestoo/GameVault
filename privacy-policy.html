Privacy Policy

At GameVault, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users' data. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of any information collected or provided through our website. Here's how we handle data:

Data Collection:
We do not collect any personal information from our users unless explicitly provided through voluntary submission forms, such as contact forms or feedback surveys. Any information voluntarily shared with us, such as email addresses, names, or other contact details, is used solely for the purpose of communication and will not be shared with third parties without prior consent.

Data Encryption:
To ensure the security of user data, our website employs industry-standard encryption protocols. All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) or Transport Layer Security (TLS) technology. This means that your information is protected from unauthorized access during transmission, providing you with a safe and secure browsing experience.

HTTPS Enforcement:
Our website enforces HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure) to further enhance security. HTTPS encrypts data exchanged between your browser and our servers, protecting it from interception or tampering by malicious third parties. You can rest assured that your interactions with our site are encrypted and secure, safeguarding your sensitive information from potential threats.

User Consent:
By using our website and voluntarily providing any personal information, you consent to the collection and use of that information as described in this privacy policy. We do not collect any information without your explicit consent, and you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

Data Usage:
Any information collected from users is used solely for the purpose for which it was provided, such as responding to inquiries, providing requested services, or improving the functionality of our website. We do not sell, rent, or lease user data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Updates to Privacy Policy:
We reserve the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes to the policy will be reflected on this page, and your continued use of our website signifies your acceptance of the revised terms.

Contact Us:
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our privacy practices or the handling of your personal information, please don't hesitate to contact us at mygamerepositoryinfo@gmail.com. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to addressing any inquiries or issues promptly and transparently.
